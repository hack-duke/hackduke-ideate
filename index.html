DUKE UNIVERSITY | APRIL 23-24, 2016

A design thinking & innovation conference

REGISTER




Empathize. Define. Ideate. Prototype. Test.



These 5 elements embody the design thinking process, a human-centric approach to innovation and problem solving that extends its applications into business and technology. IDEATE brings together students from interdisciplinary backgrounds and challenges them to use this process as a tool for manifesting beautiful and innovative ideas.


The two-day conference begins by educating students through talks and workshops hosted by industry leaders. Students are then given a chance to implement the skills they learned to build group projects with mentors. Come IDEATE with us!





SPEAKERS


Andre Tacuyan

UX DESIGNER @ GOOGLE

Andre is currently a designer at Google on the Android team. He has a strong passion to make the open and connected world of tomorrow come as seamless as possible for both human and machine. When he's not working on building his future AI wife, he likes to spend most of his time on Snapchat and YouTube. #savage

David Muñoz

USER RESEARCHER & DESIGN LEAD @ IBM

David is a user researcher and design lead for the Watson Knowledge Studio team, an upcoming product from IBM focusing on extracting concepts of interest from unstructured text. He obtained his MS in Human-Computer Interaction at Georgia Tech (’14) where he worked on several design and research projects with the CDC and Georgia WIC. Prior to obtaining his master’s, David was a psychology research assistant at Carnegie Mellon University. David is a Duke alum (’06) and worked in several cognitive psychology labs while obtaining his B.S. in Psychology.

Diógenes Brito

PRODUCT DESIGNER @ SLACK

Diogenes is a digital product designer and engineer from New York, now based in San Francisco. He holds a Bachelor’s of Science in Engineering (Product Design) degree from Stanford University, and worked at LinkedIn and Squarespace before joining Slack in 2014.

Evan Coutre

PRODUCT DESIGNER @ BOX

Evan is leading design for mobile and desktop consumer products at Box. He graduated from USC with a degree in computer science and business but gravitated to product design from his passion for art and technology.

Fiona Yeung

UX DESIGNER @ GOOGLE

Fiona Yeung is a San Francisco based interaction designer who is on Google's Material Design team. She has a particular interest in the intersection of design and technology with a focus on human-centered systems and speculating about futures. She is also an advocate for diversity in tech managing the XX+UX community.

Sahana Kumar

INTERACTION DESIGNER @ FROG DESIGN

Sahana is an interaction designer at frog, a global design and strategy firm that advances the human experience. She has a passion for learning and empathizing with all kinds of users, and helping them tell compelling stories. She graduated from Carnegie Mellon’s Design and HCI program, and moved to San Francisco, where she enjoys running around, petting dogs, and eating cheese.

Cindy Wang

UI Developer @ Capital One

Cindy is an UI developer at Capital One, where she strives to combine design thinking and engineering on an enterprise scale. Cindy graduated from Georgia Tech in Computational Media, where she cultivated an understanding of how the impact of design choices influences a user's emotions and social connections in physical and digital spaces. She finds it incredibly important to give back to the community through design, so she started by chartering a Ladies That UX chapter and mentoring in design at many hackathons.
